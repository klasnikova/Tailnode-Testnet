## Hardware Requirements
# Minimum:
- 4 or more physical CPU cores
- At least 500GB of SSD disk storage
- At least 16GB of memory (RAM)
- At least 120mbps network bandwidth
# Recommended:
- 16+ vCPUs or Intel or AMD 16 core CPU
- At least 64GB RAM
- 4TB+ nVME drives
